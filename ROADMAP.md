# Public Roadmap

This project turns AI-generated travel plans into something that can be checked, challenged, and improved before departure.

## Phase 1: Real Feedback

Timeline: day 0-14

Goals:

- Publish 14 practical posts.
- Collect 3 real or semi-real itinerary samples.
- Deliver 3 itinerary audit reports.
- Get at least 1 anonymous testimonial.

Revenue target:

- 3 test audits at 99 RMB.
- 1 candidate for a 699 RMB deep rewrite.

## Phase 2: Productization

Timeline: day 15-45

Goals:

- Move the standard audit entry price to 199 RMB.
- Build 10 reviewable case studies.
- Expand the auditor rules to 20 checks.
- Package the first self-check guide.

Revenue target:

- 3000-8000 RMB monthly revenue.
- At least 20% of leads from the website or GitHub.

## Phase 3: Personal Brand

Timeline: day 46-90

Goals:

- Maintain four public columns: Japan itinerary audit, photography travel, anime pilgrimage, and beginner low-mountain routes.
- Publish 3 public case pages.
- Package 3 self-service products.
- Open-source the itinerary auditor as a standalone tool.

Revenue target:

- 10000-20000 RMB monthly revenue.
- At least 1 creator project above 3000 RMB.

## We Do Not Do

- Pirated course or document resale.
- Crypto signals, copy-trading, custody, or return promises.
- Booking, payment, or safety guarantees.
- Public storage of private customer information.
- Fake case studies or fake testimonials.

## Weekly Public Metrics

- Posts published.
- Leads created.
- Quotes sent.
- Paid cases.
- Confirmed revenue.
- Rules added.
- Anonymous cases added.
