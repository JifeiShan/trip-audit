# Operations

Trip Audit is operated as a small, evidence-first service business. The goal is to turn public travel-risk content into qualified route-audit conversations, then into paid itinerary reviews or deeper planning projects.

## Daily Loop

1. Publish one concrete route-risk post.
2. Reply to useful comments with one clarifying question.
3. Move real leads into CRM.
4. Quote only after the route, date, group size, budget, transport assumptions, and biggest concern are clear.
5. Deliver paid audits before chasing new leads.
6. Turn one anonymous learning into the next public post.

## Offer Ladder

- Free: AI Japan itinerary 20-question checklist.
- Entry: 99 RMB test audit.
- Core: 199 RMB standard route audit.
- Premium: 699 RMB+ route rewrite.
- Creator: 3000 RMB+ travel content planning project.

## Qualification Rules

Good fit:

- The user already has a draft itinerary.
- The route involves Japan travel, winter travel, photography, anime pilgrimage, light hiking, or creator content.
- The user wants feasibility, pacing, risk, and trade-off judgment.

Bad fit:

- Booking, payment, or order handling.
- Passport, card, verification code, exact hotel, or private identity data.
- Safety guarantees, profit promises, crypto signals, or copied course files.
- Requests that need licensed legal, medical, financial, or emergency advice.
